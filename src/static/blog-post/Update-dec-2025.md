A refreshed design has just been rolled out for the S7 website, making it easier to discover what we're building and get the apps you need.

### What's New

The biggest change is the **product pages**. Each app now has dedicated space to showcase what makes it special — complete with feature breakdowns, platform availability, and direct download links.

Previously, the site gave you a quick overview of our apps, but if you wanted details, you had to hunt for them. Now everything's right there.

### The Apps We're Building

Here's what's currently available or coming soon:

#### BudgetBear - Finance Tracker
A free budgeting app to help you manage your money. Track expenses, set budgets, and get insights into your spending habits. With features like **expense tracking**, **budget planning**, **financial reports**, and **iCloud sync**, it's built to help you stay on top of your finances without the stress.

**Available now** on iOS and Mac.

#### MyMenu - Meal Planner
Plan your weekly meals, generate shopping lists automatically, and reduce food waste. MyMenu includes **weekly meal planning**, **automated shopping lists**, **meal suggestions**, and the ability to save your **favourite meals** for quick access.

**Coming soon** to iOS and Mac.
